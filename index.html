About Me

Becoming a full stack developer is truly the realization of the simple fact I love to create! Before my switch to development, I worked for years in special education. While working on my masters is special education I worked as a classroom aide working with several different amazing students. I then became a moderate/severe teacher working with students from ages 18-22. Eventually I stepped into the role of director for two different schools. It was as a director when I realized our campus lacked a database, and with no budget, I figured I'd roll up my sleeves and get to work. I ended up developing a robust system and along the way, discovered I have a true passion for coding! Now as a full stack developer I get to create use my newfound skills creating all sorts of projects. When I'm not coding, I am an amateur wood worker, musician, and theater buff. I also love most things nerdy such as video games, comics, and renaissance faires.
